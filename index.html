My Mission in 2022

Mission

There were moments when I was learning a lot, making progress, and I was excited about building a new career in software.There was a lot to learn and the road felt really long. I sometimes doubted myself and I really questioned if I was on the right career path at all.Even after I started my first real job as a developer, on most days I still felt like I had no idea what I was doing. My co-workers seemed to be way ahead of me.

Vision

My desire is to be a world class Software Developer who renders solutions for various problems, with building applications, being a great people manager and a great mentor to upcoming developers in the technology industry. I also want to use storytelling as a medium to assist other people in tech find their bearing on their journey to becoming.

Goals

As a Web developer, I want to be able to design Web pages that can effectively display material, provide interactivity, and be artistically pleasing to the user. In order to do this, you need to know the nuances of Web design and delivery and the various tools and software applications you can use.
